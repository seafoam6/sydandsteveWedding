The Rust Belt

The Rust Belt is a one-of-a-kind artist and vintage market located in downtown Ferndale. Both our wedding ceremony and reception will take place in the event space, which is located at the center of the market. The market will be open to the public until 7 p.m., so wedding guests will be able to browse through the vendors during the first part of the cocktail hour.

Parking

Metered parking is available directly behind the Rust Belt in the [Withington Street Lot](http://www.downtownferndale.com/maps-parking/area-map.html). Parking is enforced until 9 p.m. and is $.50/hour. Both metered and free on-street parking are also available nearby. Possible shuttle information coming soon.
